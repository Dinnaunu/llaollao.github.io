About LlaoLlao

We are the number one European frozen yogurt brand in the world. The project, created in 2009 by Pedro Espinosa—then 24 years old and now our CEO—began with the idea of opening a small network of stores along the Mediterranean coast. It revolutionized the sector.

In just two years, llaollao™ had opened more than 50 points of sale, and celebrated its 100th store shortly after. The following years were key for consolidating the brand nationally and internationally.

Committed to premium quality, nutritionally balanced, and fully customizable products, llaollao™ has grown to over 390 locations worldwide in just 14 years—thanks to our loyal customers and infinite topping combinations.

Our Mission & Vision

  • Mission

    To make our yogurt a product that is widely accepted for its enormous health benefits and extraordinary taste, with our customers being the inventors of their own creations—thanks to our infinite combination of toppings.

  • Vision

    To be present worldwide, consolidating a stable, successful, and lasting business model.
